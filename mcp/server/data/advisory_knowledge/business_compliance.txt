# Business Compliance - India

## Business Registration

### Types of Business Entities
1. **Sole Proprietorship**
   - Simplest form
   - No separate legal entity
   - Unlimited liability
   - Easy to set up and dissolve

2. **Partnership Firm**
   - Governed by Indian Partnership Act, 1932
   - 2-50 partners
   - Unlimited liability (except LLP)
   - Partnership deed required

3. **Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)**
   - Separate legal entity
   - Limited liability for partners
   - Minimum 2 partners
   - Governed by LLP Act, 2008

4. **Private Limited Company**
   - Separate legal entity
   - Limited liability
   - Minimum 2 directors, 2 shareholders
   - Governed by Companies Act, 2013

5. **Public Limited Company**
   - Can raise capital from public
   - Minimum 3 directors, 7 shareholders
   - Stricter compliance requirements

### Registration Process
**For Private Limited Company:**
1. Obtain Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)
2. Obtain Director Identification Number (DIN)
3. Name approval (RUN service)
4. File incorporation documents (SPICe+ form)
5. Obtain Certificate of Incorporation
6. Apply for PAN and TAN

**Timeline**: 10-15 days
**Cost**: ₹10,000 - ₹25,000 (including professional fees)

## Business Licenses and Permits

### Essential Licenses
1. **GST Registration**
   - Mandatory if turnover > ₹40 lakhs (₹20 lakhs for services)
   - Required for inter-state trade
   - Online registration through GST portal

2. **Shop and Establishment License**
   - Required for all commercial establishments
   - State-specific regulations
   - Renewal: Annual

3. **Trade License**
   - Issued by municipal corporation
   - Required for business premises
   - Renewal: Annual

4. **Professional Tax Registration**
   - State-specific
   - For businesses with employees
   - Monthly/quarterly payment

### Industry-Specific Licenses
- **Food Business**: FSSAI License
- **Manufacturing**: Factory License, Pollution Control
- **Import/Export**: IEC (Import Export Code)
- **Restaurants**: Health License, Fire Safety
- **E-commerce**: Not specifically required, but GST mandatory

## Tax Compliance

### Income Tax
- **Corporate Tax Rate**: 25% for companies with turnover < ₹400 crores
- **Advance Tax**: Quarterly payments
- **TDS Compliance**: Monthly returns
- **Annual Return**: ITR-6 for companies

### GST Compliance
- **Monthly Returns**: GSTR-1, GSTR-3B
- **Annual Return**: GSTR-9
- **Input Tax Credit**: Available for business expenses
- **Reverse Charge Mechanism**: Applicable for certain services

### Other Taxes
- **Professional Tax**: State-specific
- **Property Tax**: For business premises
- **TDS on Salaries**: Monthly compliance

## Employment Compliance

### Mandatory Registrations
1. **EPF (Employees' Provident Fund)**
   - Mandatory for establishments with 20+ employees
   - 12% employee + 12% employer contribution

2. **ESI (Employees' State Insurance)**
   - Mandatory for establishments with 10+ employees
   - Applicable if salary < ₹21,000/month

3. **Professional Tax**
   - Deducted from employee salary
   - State-specific rates

### Labor Laws
- **Shops and Establishments Act**: Working hours, leave, holidays
- **Payment of Wages Act**: Timely salary payment
- **Minimum Wages Act**: Minimum wage compliance
- **Payment of Bonus Act**: Annual bonus for eligible employees
- **Payment of Gratuity Act**: Gratuity after 5 years of service

## Annual Compliance

### For Private Limited Companies
1. **Annual General Meeting (AGM)**: Within 6 months of financial year end
2. **Annual Return (MGT-7)**: Within 60 days of AGM
3. **Financial Statements (AOC-4)**: Within 30 days of AGM
4. **Income Tax Return**: By 30th September
5. **GST Annual Return**: By 31st December
6. **Director KYC (DIR-3 KYC)**: Annually by 30th September

### Penalties for Non-Compliance
- Late filing fees
- Interest on delayed tax payments
- Prosecution for serious violations
- Disqualification of directors

## Intellectual Property

### Trademark Registration
- Protects brand name and logo
- Valid for 10 years, renewable
- Application through IP India portal
- Timeline: 12-18 months

### Copyright
- Automatic upon creation
- Registration provides legal evidence
- Valid for lifetime + 60 years

### Patent
- For inventions and innovations
- Valid for 20 years
- Application through IP India
- Timeline: 2-5 years

## Business Contracts

### Essential Contracts
1. **Shareholders Agreement**: Rights and obligations of shareholders
2. **Employment Contracts**: Terms of employment
3. **Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)**: Protect confidential information
4. **Service Agreements**: Terms with vendors/clients
5. **Lease Agreement**: For business premises

### Key Clauses
- Scope of work
- Payment terms
- Confidentiality
- Termination clause
- Dispute resolution
- Governing law

## Compliance Calendar

### Monthly
- GST returns
- TDS returns
- EPF/ESI payments
- Professional tax

### Quarterly
- Advance tax payments
- TDS returns (quarterly)

### Annual
- Income tax return
- Annual return (ROC)
- AGM
- Financial statements
- GST annual return
- EPF/ESI annual returns

## Key Recommendations

1. **Maintain proper books of accounts**
2. **File all returns on time**
3. **Keep all licenses and registrations updated**
4. **Conduct regular compliance audits**
5. **Maintain proper employment records**
6. **Protect intellectual property**
7. **Have written contracts for all transactions**
8. **Maintain statutory registers**
9. **Conduct board meetings regularly**
10. **Seek professional advice for complex matters**
